About

Arisa is a Japanese artist and animator who is currently studying at the University of Cincinnati's College of Design, Architecture, Art, and Planning (DAAP). Her major is Fine Art with pursuing Animation and Pre-Art Therapy Certificate.

She was born in Japan and moved to Cincinnati, OH when she was 12 years old. Her interest for art was born when she was a forth grade at Japanese School.

She has extensive practice with several mediums, such as oil, acrylic, graphite pencils, charcoal, etc. When she is in her free time, she enjoys using digital media and drawing her original characters.
