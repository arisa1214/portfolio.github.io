My Teddy Bear (2021)


For this animation project, I mainly focused on its storytelling.
This is a story about a boy and his teddy bear. The boy got a teddy bear for his fifth birthday. The boy and his teddy bear were always together, but one day, his teddy bear was gone while he was sleeping. He went out to find his teddy bear and he found it. At the end, he created an illustration book about him and his teddy bear and this is the end of the animation.
For this animation, I mostly rotoscoped from a couple video stock websites.
It was challenging for me to get ideas of what movements were appealing and intriguing to audiences.
