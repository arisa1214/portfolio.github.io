Falling Star (2019)


This is the first 2D animation that I have made.
I mixed three types of media for this animation, such as collage, charcoal drawing, and digital media.
The challenging part was the movement of the mermaid.

Media:
-Charcoal (Traditional medium)
-Star stickers (Traditional medium)
-Fire Alpaca (Digital medium)
-MakeAvi (Digital medium)
